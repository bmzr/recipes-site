General Tso Sauce

this is a delicious and versatile general tso sauce recipe that can be used with various dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, cornstarch, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes until well combined.
  2. Pour the mixture into a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens (about 5-7 minutes).
  4. Remove from heat and let it cool before using.
  5. This sauce can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to two weeks.

Notes

this sauce is perfect for stir-fries, as a marinade, or as a dipping sauce. adjust the level of spiciness by adding more or fewer red pepper flakes according to your taste.
