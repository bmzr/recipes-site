Hot Chocolate

This is a rich and creamy hot chocolate recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, whisk together the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add 1/4 cup of milk and stir until smooth.
  3. Gradually add the remaining milk, whisking to combine.
  4. Heat over medium heat until hot, but not boiling.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.

Notes

This hot chocolate can be customized with your favorite toppings, such as whipped cream or marshmallows. Enjoy!
